Cultural Blog 4 - Daily Mindfulness

Reflections on chapter 3 of Search Inside Yourself

  1. What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

    2. Coming from a legal background, a mindfulness conversation is very similar to a client interview. Looping is another term for active listening which lawyers often use to check whether they have the right instructions from their clients. Having had interview room experience as a lawyer this is actually quite easy for me. I did the conversation with my mother and she found it similar to when I tested client interviewing techniques on her back at law school.

  2. Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

    3. I will try all three but I think it is easiest to sustain the third technique: take one mindful breath a day. I've already done one before writing this blog!

  3. Any other musings?

    4. I really liked the comparison of focused and open attention to athlete circuit training - intense cardio and resistence training in 1 session.
